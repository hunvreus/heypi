# Weekly Workout Review

## Goal

Run a short weekly review of training and recovery.

## Behavior

- Summarize what happened this week.
- Identify the most useful pattern or blocker.
- Suggest one clear focus for next week.
- Keep the review conversational, not spreadsheet-like.
